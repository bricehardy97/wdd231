Yankton Goose Hunts - Site Plan

Site Name

Yankton Goose Hunts

This name reflects the location (Yankton, SD) and the focus of the business—guided goose hunting. It is clear, memorable, and keyword-friendly for search engines.

Optional domain availability: yankton-goosehunts.com

Site Purpose

The website will provide detailed information on guided goose hunting packages, including lodging and meal options. Visitors can view package pricing, check seasonal availability, and request bookings. The site will also showcase past hunts and customer testimonials to build trust and attract new hunters.

Scenarios

  • What are the available goose hunting packages and what do they include?
  • Where will I stay and what kind of meals are included with each package?

Color Schema

  • Dark Slate Gray – Used for main headings and primary navigation background.
  • Dark Goldenrod – Used as an accent for buttons, borders, and links.

Typography

  • Merriweather – Used for headings to give a rustic, classic feel.
  • Open Sans – Used for body text for readability and modern clarity.
